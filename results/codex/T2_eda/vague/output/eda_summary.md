# EDA Summary

Dataset size: 32,561 rows x 15 original columns.

Key findings:
- Income is imbalanced: 24.08% of records are `>50K`.
- Higher earners are older on average (44.25 vs 36.78 years).
- Higher earners also work more hours on average (45.47 vs 38.84 per week).
- The `>50K` rate differs sharply by sex: Male 30.57% vs Female 10.95%.
- Capital gains are uncommon overall but much more common among high earners (21.39% vs 4.19%).
- Highest observed `>50K` rates among larger occupations:
- Exec-managerial: 48.40% (`n`=4066)
- Prof-specialty: 44.90% (`n`=4140)
- Protective-serv: 32.51% (`n`=649)

Race-level >50K rates:
- Asian-Pac-Islander: 26.56%
- White: 25.59%
- Black: 12.39%
- Amer-Indian-Eskimo: 11.58%
- Other: 9.23%
